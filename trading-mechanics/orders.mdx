### Additional Tips for Effective Trading

- **Monitor Market Conditions**: Always consider current market trends and news that might affect asset prices before placing an order.
- **Manage Risks**: Use the liquidation price information to set appropriate stop-loss orders and manage your exposure.
- **Review Terms**: Ensure you understand all the terms associated with trading on LemonX, including fees and liquidity conditions.

By following these steps and considerations, you can effectively utilize LemonX's trading platform to maximize your trading performance while managing risks.
